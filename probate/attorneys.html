For Probate Attorneys

Real estate support for probate attorneys and their clients.

A probate-focused real estate resource for attorneys who want clients to have clear guidance on inherited property, valuation, sale preparation, and next steps throughout the Greater Houston Area.

What I can provide

Support can include current market valuations, as-is versus prep-for-market recommendations, vendor coordination, property preparation guidance, and consistent communication with families, title, and the professionals involved in the transaction.

Referral-friendly resources

The executor guide, valuation page, and probate resource pages can be shared directly with clients who are unsure how to approach inherited real estate and need a practical starting point.

Why this helps attorneys

Probate matters often involve real estate questions that sit outside the legal work itself but still affect the overall experience for the family. When clients have a clear path for valuation, property condition, sale options, and coordination, the process tends to move with fewer delays and less confusion.

That support can be especially helpful when a home is vacant, multiple heirs are involved, repairs are being debated, or the family simply needs a realistic plan before deciding how to proceed.

What referred clients can expect

Referred clients can expect straightforward communication, a practical market-based opinion of value, and guidance that reflects the condition of the property and the goals of the estate. The focus is on helping families make informed decisions without adding unnecessary pressure or complication.

When needed, support can also extend to helping coordinate the right service providers, whether that means cleanup, light repairs, landscaping, estate sale assistance, or title-related coordination.

As a Certified Probate Real Estate Specialist, I understand the importance of working in alignment with the attorney's guidance, not around it, so clients receive practical real estate support that fits the broader probate process.
